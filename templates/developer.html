Important Indian Government Health Websites

  • Ministry of Health and Family Welfare (MoHFW): https://www.mohfw.gov.in

    Provides official information about national health policies, programs, COVID-19 updates, and public health initiatives.

  • National Health Portal (NHP): https://www.nhp.gov.in

    A comprehensive portal offering information about diseases, health tips, government schemes, and health facilities.

  • Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY): https://pmjay.gov.in

    Details about the Ayushman Bharat health insurance scheme, eligibility checks, and empaneled hospitals.

  • Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS): https://cghs.gov.in

    Provides information about healthcare services for government employees and pensioners.

  • Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR): https://www.icmr.gov.in

    Updates on medical research, disease surveillance, and health data in India.

  • National Digital Health Mission (NDHM)/ABHA: https://healthid.ndhm.gov.in

    Platform for creating and managing a digital health ID to store medical records.

  • Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI): https://www.fssai.gov.in

    Information on food safety standards, nutrition, and guidelines for a healthy lifestyle.

  • Rashtriya Bal Swasthya Karyakram (RBSK): https://rbsk.gov.in

    Provides details on child health screening and intervention services under the National Health Mission.

  • National AIDS Control Organization (NACO): http://naco.gov.in

    Resources and support for HIV/AIDS awareness, treatment, and prevention.

  • All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS): https://www.aiims.edu

    Access to research, medical services, and hospital facilities provided by AIIMS.

  • National Tuberculosis Elimination Program (NTEP): https://tbcindia.gov.in

    Information on TB elimination strategies, diagnosis, and treatment facilities.

  • Aarogya Setu: https://aarogyasetu.gov.in

    COVID-19 tracking, contact tracing, and vaccination updates.

  • Jan Aushadhi Scheme: https://janaushadhi.gov.in

    Provides information on affordable generic medicines and locations of Jan Aushadhi Kendras.

  • Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA): https://pmsma.nhp.gov.in

    Focuses on providing comprehensive antenatal care for pregnant women.

  • Health Management Information System (HMIS): https://hmis.nhp.gov.in

    Data on health indicators, maternal and child health, and government health initiatives.
